Takoyaki Recipe

Description

This recipe will show you how to make a simple serving of Takoyaki. For this recipe, you will need a Takoyaki grill.

Ingredients

  • Takoyaki mix
  • Cooked octopus
  • Red pickled ginger
  • Green onion
  • Tempura scraps
  • Takoyaki sauce
  • Dried green seaweed
  • Dried bonito flakes

Steps

  1. Apply 1/4 inch(5mm) of oil to each hole in the takoyaki grill
  2. Once smoke comes out of the grill, fill each hole with batter and the octopus filling
  3. Once the bottom of the takoyaki ball is crispy, rotate it 90 degrees to let the uncooked batter pour out into the hole
  4. Once all sides are cooked, set the takoyaki in a plate and enjoy!

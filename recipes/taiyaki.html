Taiyaki Recipe

Description

This recipe will show you how to make 8 pieces of Taiyaki. For this recipe, you will need a Taiyaki Pan.

Ingredients

  • 170 grams of butter (6 ounces, 12 tablespoons)
  • 65 grams light brown sugar (2.3 ounces, 8 tablespoons)
  • 100 grams egg (2 eggs, 3.5 ounces)
  • 240 grams cake flour (8.5 ounces, 1 1/2 cup)
  • 6 grams baking powder (0.2 ounces, 2 teaspoons)
  • 155 grams warm milk (5.5 ounces, 2/3 cup)
  • 10 grams honey (0.35 ounces, 2 teaspoons)
  • 6 grams light soy sauce (0.2 ounces, 2 teaspoons)
  • 4 grams vanilla extract (0.14 ounces, 2 teaspoons)
  • Red bean paste

Steps

  1. Mix together the milk, honey, soy sauce, and vanilla extract and set aside.
  2. Cream the butter together with the sugar
  3. Beat in one egg, then the other
  4. Beat in half the flour, then the milk, then the other half of the flour until the batter's form is fluid but not liquid.
  5. Heat taiyaki pan between low and medium-low
  6. Squeeze pool of batter into the body, then the tail, then the body again
  7. Place red bean paste into the center of the taiyaki and press in halfway
  8. Pour more batter over the red bean paste so that it's fully covered
  9. Close lid and begin to grill until both sides are evenly browned. The process should take about 3 minutes for each side, but timing may vary.
  10. Remove taiyaki from pan and enjoy!

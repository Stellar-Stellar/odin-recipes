Obanyaki Recipe

Description

This recipe will show you how to make 6 obanyaki pancakes.

Ingredients

    Pancakes

  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 5 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
  • 7/8 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • Custard Cream

  • 5 teaspoons flour
  • 4 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  • 7/8 cup of milk
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 teaspoons butter

    • Red Bean Filling

  • 1 cup Japanese red beans
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Steps

    Preparing cream filling

  1. Mix sugar anf flour well with egg yolk in a small saucepan, gradually adding milk until the consistency is pasty
  2. Cook on low heat until thickened into a cream consistency, stirring pan over the heat.
  3. Once there is a cream consistency, take the pan off the heat and add butter and vanilla extract.
  4. Filter the custard creme in a sieve. Keep cool in the refrigerator.

    5. Preparing red bean filling

  5. Whip 1/4 cup of heavy cream until there are stiff peaks
  6. Mix in the can of Japanese red beans and keep cool in the refrigerator

    7. Preparing pancakes

  7. Sift the flour and the baking powder together in a small bowl
  8. In a separate bowl, mix sugar and eggs, until a thick consistency
  9. Add honey, vegetable oil and milk into the egg mixture. Then, add in the flour and baking powder mixture.
  10. Preheat a griddle pan to 150C. Pour the batter into a small round shape with a diameter of approximately 8 cm. Once a bubble surfaces on the top, flip the pancake until both browns sides are golden brown.
  11. Cover pancakes to prevent them from drying out. Once they have cooled off, add the fillings prepared above and enjoy!
